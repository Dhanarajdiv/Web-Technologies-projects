Movie Review: "Dasara"

Synopsis

Dasara is a raw and gritty action-drama set in the rustic backdrop of Veerlapally,a village near Singareni coal mines in Telangana.

Ratings

IMDb: 7.8/10

Rotten Tomatoes: 75%

Review

Dasara is a gritty and intense action drama set against the backdrop of rural Telangana, specially the coal-laden village of Veerlapally. Directed by Srikanth Odela in his debut, the film showcases Nani in a never-seen-before avatar, delivering one of his career-best performances.

Cast and Crew

  • Director:Srikanth Odela
  • Stars: Nani, Keerthy Suresh, Dheekshith Shetty, Shine Tom Chacko
  • Writer: Srikanth Odela
  • Composer: Santhosh Narayanan
